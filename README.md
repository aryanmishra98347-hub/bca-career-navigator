# 🎓 BCA Training & Career Guidance Platform

A modern, data-driven, and student-focused web platform designed to help **BCA 2nd semester students** make informed decisions about their training programs and future career paths.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Choosing the right training program early in your BCA journey can significantly impact your career. This platform provides **clear, structured, and practical insights** into various training domains such as:

* Web Development
* Data Science
* Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
* Cybersecurity
* App Development
* Cloud Computing

The goal is simple:
👉 **Help students understand what to choose, what to learn, and how to succeed.**

---

## 🎯 Key Features

### 📚 Training Program Insights

Each training program is explained in detail with:

* Real-world applications
* Industry use cases
* Job roles (entry → advanced)
* Demand level in the market

---

### 📈 Career Growth & Salary Analysis

* Year-wise career trajectory
* Salary progression (India-focused data)
* Graphical representation of growth trends
* Comparison across different fields

---

### 🧠 Skill Stack Breakdown

Clear distinction between:

* **Core Skills** (must-have fundamentals)
* **Advanced Skills** (industry-level expertise)

Also includes:

* Tools & technologies
* Frameworks
* Practical learning roadmap

---

### 🛣️ Step-by-Step Career Roadmap

* What to learn during training
* What extra skills are needed to secure a job
* Structured learning path (Beginner → Advanced)

---

### ⚖️ Pros & Cons Analysis

Honest evaluation of each field:

* Advantages
* Challenges
* Work-life expectations
* Future scope

---

### 📊 Interactive Data Visualization

* Salary growth charts
* Field demand comparison
* Skill importance graphs

---

### 🔍 Smart Exploration Features

* Search and filter training programs
* Compare multiple career paths
* Clean and intuitive navigation

---

### 🌙 Modern UI/UX

* Fully responsive design
* Smooth animations and transitions
* Minimal and professional layout
* Dark mode support

---
